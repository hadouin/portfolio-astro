## Overview

Centime is a branding project where I designed the logo and core visual identity. The mark aims for a clean, confident look that works across digital and print touchpoints.

## Deliverables

- Primary logo and wordmark
- App icon / favicon
- Brand banner
- Color palette and typography guidelines

## Process

The identity was developed through iterative sketching and refinement, prioritizing legibility at small sizes and a distinctive silhouette that stands out against crowded brand contexts.
